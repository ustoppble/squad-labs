Focus on People, Not Paper

Disability providers know firsthand the challenges facing individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities — social isolation, communication barriers, limited resources, and the constant struggle for independence. Still, outdated systems and never-ending compliance requirements shift your focus, limiting the time and energy you have to support meaningful progress.

Kibu was founded to help disability providers overcome these hurdles to focus on what really matters: supporting individuals living with IDD.
